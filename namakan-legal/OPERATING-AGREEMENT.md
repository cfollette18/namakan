## ARTICLE 3 - PURPOSE

3.1 **Purpose.** The purpose of the Company is to engage in AI consulting services, including but not limited to:
- Artificial intelligence strategy and implementation consulting
- AI workflow design and optimization

- AI integration and deployment services

## ARTICLE 4 - MANAGEMENT

4.1 **Member Management.** The Member shall manage the Company and shall have full authority to make all management decisions, including but not limited to:
- Opening bank accounts
- Entering contracts
- Hiring employees and contractors
- Acquiring and disposing of assets
- Filing tax returns

4.2 **Day-to-Day Operations.** The Member may delegate day-to-day operations to agents or employees as deemed appropriate.

---

## ARTICLE 5 - FINANCIAL MATTERS

5.1 **Profits and Losses.** All profits and losses of the Company shall accrue to the Member.

5.2 **Distributions.** The Member may make distributions at such times and in such amounts as determined in their sole discretion.

5.3 **Taxation.** The Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal and state income tax purposes. The Member shall report all income and expenses of the Company on their individual tax returns.

5.4 **Books and Records.** The Company shall maintain accurate books and records, which shall be kept at the principal place of business.

---

## ARTICLE 6 - LIABILITY

6.1 **Limited Liability.** The Member's liability for the debts and obligations of the Company shall be limited to the extent provided under applicable law.

6.2 **Personal Liability.** The Member shall not be personally liable for any Company obligation solely by reason of being a member, except as otherwise provided by law.

---

## ARTICLE 7 - DISSOLUTION

7.1 **Dissolution Events.** The Company shall dissolve upon the earliest of:
- The Member's written decision to dissolve
- The death, resignation, or incapacity of the Member
- The entry of a decree of judicial dissolution

7.2 **Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall:
- Wind up the Company's affairs
- Pay or make provision for Company's debts and liabilities
- Distribute remaining assets to the Member

---

## ARTICLE 8 - GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of **Minnesota**.

8.2 **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by the Member.

8.3 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

8.4 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire operating agreement of the Company.
