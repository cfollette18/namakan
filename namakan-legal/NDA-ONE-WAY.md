## ARTICLE 1 - DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed by Disclosing Party to Receiving Party, whether orally, in writing, electronically, by demonstration, or otherwise, including but not limited to:

- Business plans, strategies, financial information, and projections
- Customer lists, pricing, and contract terms
- Technical information, designs, specifications, and data
- Trade secrets, know-how, and proprietary methodologies
- Product plans, roadmaps, and features
- Software, source code, and algorithms
- AI prompts, frameworks, training data, and outputs
- Marketing strategies and materials
- Any information marked or identified as "confidential" or "proprietary"

1.2 **Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement;
(b) Was rightfully known to Receiving Party prior to disclosure;
(c) Is independently developed by Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information;
(d) Is lawfully obtained from a third party who has the right to disclose it;
(e) Is required to be disclosed by law or regulation, provided that Receiving Party gives Disclosing Party prompt written notice to allow seeking protective measures.

---

## ARTICLE 2 - OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

2.1 **Use Restrictions.** Receiving Party shall:
- Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- Not use Confidential Information for any competitive, commercial, or other purpose
- Not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any products or materials provided

2.2 **Disclosure Restrictions.** Receiving Party shall:
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
- Not copy or reproduce Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose
- Limit disclosure to employees, contractors, and advisors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations

2.3 **Standard of Care.** Receiving Party shall protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care.

2.4 **Permitted Disclosure.** Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information as required by law, regulation, or court order, provided that Receiving Party:
- Gives Disclosing Party prompt written notice upon learning of such requirement
- Cooperates with Disclosing Party's reasonable efforts to seek protective measures
- Discloses only the minimum necessary to comply

---

## ARTICLE 3 - OWNERSHIP

3.1 **Retention of Rights.** Disclosing Party retains all right, title, and interest in and to its Confidential Information. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any ownership rights.

3.2 **No License.** This Agreement does not grant Receiving Party any license or right to use Disclosing Party's Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose.

## ARTICLE 4 - TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.** This Agreement shall remain in effect for [TWO (2) / THREE (3)] years from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated.

4.2 **Termination.** Either party may terminate this Agreement upon [THIRTY (30)] days' written notice.

4.3 **Obligations Upon Termination.** Upon termination or upon Disclosing Party's request, Receiving Party shall:
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession
- Remove Confidential Information from any AI systems or tools used
- Certify in writing that such return or destruction has been completed

4.4 **Survival.** Articles 3, 5, and 6 shall survive termination.
