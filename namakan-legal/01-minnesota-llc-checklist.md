# Minnesota LLC Formation Checklist
## Namakan AI Consulting, LLC

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Filing Preparation

### Name Requirements
- [ ] Choose a unique LLC name (must include "Limited Liability Company" or "LLC")
- [ ] Verify name availability via Minnesota Secretary of State [business search](https://mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us/UCC/Search)
- [ ] Reserve name if desired (optional, valid for 12 months) - $50 fee
- [ ] Ensure "Namakan" or chosen name doesn't conflict with existing trademarks

### Registered Agent
- [ ] Designate a Minnesota registered agent (individual or registered agent service)
- [ ] Agent must have physical address in Minnesota
- [ ] Agent accepts legal documents on behalf of the LLC

### Initial Members/Managers
- [ ] Identify all LLC members (owners)
- [ ] Determine management structure (member-managed vs. manager-managed)
- [ ] Draft Operating Agreement (internal governance document)

---

## Phase 2: Filing Documents

### Articles of Organization (Minnesota Statutes Chapter 322B)
- [ ] File Articles of Organization with Minnesota Secretary of State
- [ ] Include required information:
  - [ ] LLC name
  - [ ] Registered agent name and address
  - [ ] Principal place of business address
  - [ ] Management structure (member or manager)
  - [ ] Name and address of each organizer
  - [ ] Duration (perpetual unless specified otherwise)
- [ ] Filing fee: $100 (online) / $110 (paper)
- [ ] Processing time: 3-5 business days (online), 2-3 weeks (paper)

### Optional Filings
- [ ] File for a Registered Office address (if different from principal place of business)
- [ ] Obtain Foreign Qualification (if doing business in other states)

---

## Phase 3: Post-Formation Requirements

### Internal Documents
- [ ] Draft and execute Operating Agreement (all members sign)
- [ ] Draft member/manager resolution for opening bank account
- [ ] Document initial capital contributions

### EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS via [IRS.gov](https://www.irs.gov/entities/basic-requirements-for-llcs) (free, immediate online)
- [ ] File Form SS-4 if applying by mail/fax

### Business Licenses & Registrations
- [ ] Check Minnesota business license requirements at [mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us](https://mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us/)
- [ ] Register for Minnesota tax IDs (if applicable):
  - [ ] Minnesota Tax ID (for sales/use tax)
  - [ ] Withholding tax (if hiring employees)
- [ ] Check local city/county business license requirements
- [ ] Verify professional licenses (AI consulting - generally no special license required)

### Insurance
- [ ] Obtain General Liability Insurance
- [ ] Consider Professional Liability / Errors & Omissions (E&O) Insurance
- [ ] Consider Cyber Liability Insurance
- [ ] Workers' Compensation (if hiring employees)

### Banking
- [ ] Open business checking account (bring EIN, Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement)
- [ ] Set up business credit card
- [ ] Configure accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.)

---

## Phase 4: Ongoing Compliance

### Annual Requirements (Minnesota)
- [ ] File Annual Renewal with Secretary of State - $0 (no annual report fee in MN)
- [ ] Update any changed information within 30 days
- [ ] Maintain registered agent at all times
- [ ] Keep proper records of meetings and decisions (if member-managed)

### Tax Obligations
- [ ] Understand pass-through taxation (LLC itself doesn't pay income tax)
- [ ] File Form 1065 (Partnership Return) if multi-member, or Schedule C with Form 1040 if single-member
- [ ] Consider S-C election (Form 2553) if beneficial
- [ ] Track quarterly estimated tax payments
- [ ] Collect and remit sales tax if applicable

### Record Keeping
- [ ] Maintain financial records separate from personal
- [ ] Keep copies of all contracts and legal documents
- [ ] Document major business decisions
- [ ] Store records at principal place of business
