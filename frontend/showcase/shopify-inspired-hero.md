# Shopify-Inspired Hero Image Prompt

## Concept
A clean, professional hero image that captures the essence of Shopify's aesthetic but for AI/manufacturing.

## Design Direction
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, high-end SaaS
- **Color**: White base with teal (#0D9488) accents
- **Vibe**: Professional, trustworthy, modern
- **Text**: None (image only)

## Image Prompt

```
Full-width hero section illustration for Namakan custom AI engineering company website.

Design: Clean Shopify-inspired aesthetic, professional SaaS style.

LEFT SIDE (60% of image):
- Large bold headline text: "AI Built for Manufacturers"
- Short subtext below: "Custom workflows. Real results."
- Big teal CTA button: "Talk to Us →"
- Below headline: 3 small stat badges:
  - "50+ Automations Built"
  - "Manufacturing Focus"  
  - "Deploys in Weeks"

RIGHT SIDE (40% of image):
- Clean illustration showing AI workflow concept:
  - 3 floating cards/panels connected by glowing teal lines
  - Card 1: Document icon + "Your Data"
  - Card 2: AI brain/gears icon + "Custom AI"
  - Card 3: Checkmark + "Your Workflow"
  - Arrows flowing between cards showing automation

BOTTOM LEFT:
- Subtle logo mark: "AMAKAN" text next to abstract N symbol

STYLE:
- Clean white background
- Teal (#0D9488) as primary accent
- Subtle shadows on cards
- Minimal, professional, trustworthy
- Like Shopify, Stripe, Linear hero sections
- No busy patterns, clean typography feel

The overall feeling: "This is a professional company that builds real AI solutions for real manufacturers."
```

## Variations to Generate

### Variation 2: Product Showcase
Show all 4 products as floating cards in a grid layout with the headline overlay.

### Variation 3: Comparison Focus
Emphasize the "Before/After" or "Generic AI vs Namakan" comparison visually with two sides.

## Usage
This image would go in the hero section of namakanai.com, replacing or supplementing the current comparison cards.
