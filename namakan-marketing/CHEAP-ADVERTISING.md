# Cheap Advertising Strategy — Namakan AI Engineering

*Zero-to-$5K/month budget. Focus on what actually works for B2B AI consulting.*

---

## The Philosophy

For a bootstrapped AI consulting firm, **every dollar goes into channels that build credibility and generate warm leads**. Paid ads are a last resort. The strategy:

1. **Content** — prove you know AI (free)
2. **LinkedIn** — reach decision-makers directly (free)
3. **Cold email** — scalable outreach (almost free)
4. **Local SEO** — get found when searches find you (free)
5. **Referrals** — your best clients bring more clients (free)

---

## 1. LinkedIn — The #1 Channel for B2B AI

**Cost**: $0–$50/month (Sales Navigator is $99/mo but you can start free)

LinkedIn is where B2B decision-makers live. Namakan's sweet spot: **operations VPs, CTOs, and founders** at 50-500 person companies in the Minneapolis/North Metro area.

### What to Post (3-5x per week)

**Content that works for AI consulting:**
- "Here's what most companies get wrong about AI fine-tuning..."
- "We just finished a 3-week RAG pipeline for a healthcare client..."
- "Unpopular opinion: You probably don't need GPT-5. You need better data."
- Behind-the-scenes of a project (anonymized)
- Industry takes: "Why manufacturing companies in Minnesota are uniquely positioned to benefit from AI..."
- Case study snippets (without revealing client)

**Format that wins:**
- Short paragraph + 3-5 bullet points
- One clear insight per post
- No hashtags (or max 2)
- End with a question to drive comments

**Sample post template:**
```
Most companies spending $50K+ on AI projects are solving the wrong problem.

They're buying the latest model. What they actually need:
• Better training data
• Cleaner RAG pipelines
• Agents that know THEIR business

We just finished a fine-tuning project for a medical device company.
Their old model: generic, hallucinating, useless.
Their new model: answers questions about THEIR products, THEIR processes.

The fix wasn't the model. It was the context.

What's the hardest AI problem in YOUR industry?
```

### LinkedIn Outreach (Free)

- Connect with 10-15 relevant prospects per day
- Personalize every connection request: reference their company, role, or a post they made
- After connecting, send a value-add message (not a sales pitch)

### Sales Navigator (~$99/mo)

Worth it once you have budget. Lets you:
- Target exact company sizes, industries, titles
- Get lead recommendations
- Track prospects' activity

## 2. Cold Email — Scalable and Almost Free

**Cost**: $0–$50/month (brevo.com free up to 500 contacts, or use Gmail + Mailtrack)

You already have the cold email sequence in `namakan-sales/COLD-EMAIL-SEQUENCE.md`. Now execute it.

### The Stack

| Tool | Cost | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) | Free to 500 contacts | Email sending + tracking |
| Apollo.io | Free tier | Prospect research + verified emails |
| LinkedIn Sales Navigator | $99/mo | Find decision-makers |

### Execution

**Week 1-2**: Research 100 targets using Apollo
- Target: CTOs, VPs of Engineering, Founders
- Industry: Healthcare, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Legal
- Location: Minneapolis-North Metro, MN
- Company size: 50-500 employees

**Week 3**: Upload to Brevo, launch cold email sequence
- 5-email sequence (you already have this)
- Emails 2-3 days apart
- Track opens and clicks

**Week 4**: Follow up on responders
- Respond within 2 hours
- Book a discovery call

### Email Best Practices

- Send from a personal email (not noreply@)
- Keep subject lines under 50 chars, curiosity-driven
- First line of email is critical — reference THEM, not you
- No attachments in first email (spam filter)
- Plain text emails (no HTML heavy templates)

## 3. Content Marketing — Prove You Know AI

**Cost**: $0 (your time)

Every piece of content is a credibility builder and an SEO asset. Publish on:
- LinkedIn (primary)
- Namakan website blog (secondary)
- Medium or Substack (optional)

### Content Ideas That Convert

1. **"What is fine-tuning and when does it make sense?"** — Educational, captures search traffic
2. **"We built a custom AI employee for a [industry] company. Here's what we learned."** — Case study
3. **"Why RAG beats fine-tuning for most companies"** — Contrarian take, gets shares
4. **"The AI readiness assessment checklist"** — Lead magnet
5. **"3 ways AI is destroying productivity in [industry]"** — Problem-aware content

### Technical Blog Posts

Good for SEO and for establishing expertise:
- "How we fine-tuned a Qwen model for a medical device company using Google Colab"
- "Building a production RAG pipeline with ChromaDB and LangGraph"
- "Why we use LoRA instead of full fine-tuning for 90% of clients"

## 4. Local SEO — Get Found When It Matters

**Cost**: $0

When a company in Blaine or Minneapolis searches "AI consulting Minneapolis" or "custom AI fine-tuning Minnesota", Namakan should show up.

### Free Local SEO Actions

1. **Google Business Profile** (free)
   - Create one at business.google.com
   - Fill every field: description, services, hours, photos
   - Get reviews from clients
   - Post updates weekly

2. **Yelp + Bing Places** (free)
   - Same listing, multiple places

3. **Website SEO basics** (free, do it once)
   - Title tags: "AI Consulting | Custom Fine-Tuning | Namakan — Blaine, MN"
   - Meta description: "Namakan builds custom AI systems for Minnesota businesses. Fine-tuned models, RAG pipelines, and AI employees. Serving Minneapolis, St. Paul, and North Metro."
   - Schema markup for local business
   - 5-10 blog posts targeting local + AI keywords

4. **Directory listings** (free)
   - Get listed on:
     - Minneapolis Regional Chamber
     - Minnesota Chamber of Commerce
     - Alignable (local business network)
     - BBB (free basic listing)

## 5. Referrals — Your Secret Weapon

**Cost**: $0 (but consider a referral fee)

For a $5K-$50K service, referrals are gold. Nobody refers a bad vendor to a friend.

### How to Get Referrals

1. **Ask every client explicitly**: "Do you know anyone else who might benefit from this?"
2. **Give referral bonus**: "We'll give you 10% off your next project for every referral that becomes a client"
3. **Strategic partners**: CPA firms, marketing agencies, law firms — they see companies that need AI
4. **Build relationships with IT consultants** in Minneapolis who don't do AI

### Partner Channels

| Partner | Why they refer |
|---------|---------------|
| CPA firms | Clients asking about AI tools |
| Marketing agencies | Need AI integration for clients |
| IT consultants | Clients need custom AI |
| Business coaches | Clients scaling operations |

## 6. Free / Cheap Paid Channels

### Worth It Under $100/month

| Channel | Cost | Why |
|---------|------|-----|
| **LinkedIn posts** | $0 | Reach decision-makers organically |
| **Brevo email** | $0-50 | Cold email outreach |
| **Apollo.io** | Free tier | Email finding + verification |
| **Google Business Profile** | $0 | Local SEO |
| **NextDoor ads** | $50/mo | Local B2B surprisingly effective |

### Probably Not Worth It

| Channel | Why |
|---------|-----|
| Facebook/Instagram ads | B2B decision-makers aren't there |
| Google Search ads | Too expensive for AI consulting keywords ($20+/click) |
| Trade shows | Too expensive for early-stage |
| PR / press releases | Hard to get without a story |
