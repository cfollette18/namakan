## 1. Brand Positioning

### 1.1 Tagline & Core Messaging

**Primary Tagline:**
> **"Agents that DO, not just TALK."**

**Supporting Taglines:**
- "Your AI workforce that works 24/7"
- "Autonomous operations for local businesses"
- "AI workers — not chatbots"

**One-Line Pitch:**
> "We build autonomous AI agents for local businesses — AI workers that complete tasks 24/7, not chatbots that answer questions."

**Messaging Framework:**

| Message Level | Content |
|---------------|---------|
| **One-Liner** | Namakan builds autonomous AI agents for local businesses — AI workers that complete tasks 24/7. |
| **Problem** | Local businesses are buried in repetitive tasks: returns, scheduling, follow-ups, data entry. They don't have time for AI — and chatbot vendors sell tools, not results. |
| **Solution** | We deploy autonomous AI agents that DO the work — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Not an assistant that answers questions. A worker that completes tasks. |
| **Proof** | Blaine manufacturers saving 15+ hours/week. Healthcare practices cutting no-show rates by 28%. Restaurants handling takeout orders while they sleep. |

### 1.2 Value Proposition

**Core Value Prop:**
> **"Namakan deploys autonomous AI agents — not chatbots — that complete the repetitive work eating up your team's time, so you can focus on growing your business."**

**Value Pillars:**

1. **Task Completion, Not Just Answers** — Our agents DO the work, not just respond to questions
2. **24/7 AI Workforce** — Agents that never sleep, never call in sick, never miss a follow-up
3. **Fast Implementation** — Most agents live in 2 weeks or less, not months
4. **Local & Personal** — Face-to-face available in the north metro. Not a faceless SaaS.
5. **Transparent Pricing** — Fixed quotes, no surprise invoices, no lock-in

### 1.3 Differentiation Narrative

**The Story:**
> "Local businesses in Blaine and the north metro are overwhelmed. Not by big strategic decisions — by the same small tasks over and over. Processing returns. Following up on leads. Scheduling appointments. Answering 'where's my order?'
>
> Enterprise AI companies ignore these businesses. Chatbot vendors sell tools that answer questions. Nobody is building AI WORKERS that complete the tasks.
>
> Namakan does. We deploy autonomous agents — AI workers — that handle the work end-to-end, 24/7. Your agent processes that return, books that appointment, and sends that follow-up. Done.
>
> That's the difference. Not a chatbot that describes what you do. An agent that does the work."

**Positioning Statement:**
> For local Blaine and north metro businesses who are tired of repetitive tasks consuming their team's time, Namakan is the AI consulting firm that deploys autonomous agents — not chatbots — that complete tasks 24/7, integrate with existing systems, and deliver results in weeks, not months.

## 2. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 2.1 Target Customer Segments (ICP)

**Primary ICP: Local SMBs — Blaine and North Metro Minneapolis**

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Company Size** | 5–50 employees |
| **Revenue** | $500K–$10M |
| **Industry** | Manufacturing, Healthcare, Retail, Restaurants, Professional Services |
| **Owner/Operator** | Owner-led or small management team |
| **Pain Points** | Repetitive tasks, staff burnout, slow response times, missed follow-ups |
| **Decision Maker** | Owner, Office Manager, Operations Manager |

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Scorecard:**

| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Clear repetitive task | 25% | Can we name the task the agent will handle? |
| Budget authority | 20% | $500+/mo for the right solution |
| Decision maker access | 20% | Can we reach the owner/director? |
| Timeline < 60 days | 15% | Urgency to solve the problem |
| Existing tech stack | 10% | Do they have the basics (email, CRM, website)? |
| Cultural fit | 10% | Open to trying something new? |
| **Total** | 100% | |

**Secondary Segment: Mid-size businesses in Twin Cities metro**
- 50–200 employees
- More complex workflows = larger agent deployments
- Longer sales cycle but higher deal value

### 2.2 Industry Vertical Priority

**Primary Vertical: Manufacturing**
- High repetitive task volume (scheduling, inventory, quotes, follow-ups)
- 15+ hours/week typically automatable
- Receptive to efficiency gains
- **Target:** 30% of early customers

**Secondary Vertical: Healthcare / Medical**
- Front desk overload, appointment management, patient follow-ups
- 28%+ no-show rates are common and costly
- Strong ROI case for autonomous agents
- **Target:** 25% of early customers

**Tertiary Verticals:**
- Retail (20%) — Returns, order tracking, follow-ups
- Restaurants (15%) — Reservations, takeout orders, reviews
- Professional Services (10%) — Scheduling, document processing

### 2.3 Geographic Focus

**Priority Territory:**

| Priority | Region | Rationale |

## 3. Marketing Channels

### 3.1 Referral Marketing (Primary)

**Why it works first:**
- Local businesses trust peer recommendations
- Agent results are tangible and visible
- Referral fee creates incentive

**Referral Program:**
- 20% of first month's revenue for any referred client
- Formalize with a simple referral agreement
- Target: CPAs, web agencies, IT firms, other local business advisors

**Tactics:**
1. Ask every client at delivery: "Do you know another business owner who'd benefit from this?"
2. Formal referral program with written agreement
3. Annual review meeting = natural referral opportunity

### 3.2 Local Networking

**Priority Channels:**

| Channel | Why | Frequency |
|---------|-----|----------|
| Chamber of Commerce events | Local credibility, peer referrals | Monthly |
| BNI chapters | Structured referral networking | Weekly |
| Industry-specific meetups | Manufacturing, healthcare events | Monthly |
| SCORE / SBDC workshops | Establish authority, warm leads | Quarterly |

### 3.3 LinkedIn Content

**Content Pillars:**

| Pillar | Goal | Examples |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Agent completion stories** | Show what agents DO | "Here's how our agent processed 47 returns while the owner slept" |
| **Local business wins** | Social proof | Case studies from Blaine/north metro businesses |
| **AI vs. agents** | Differentiation | "We don't build chatbots. Here's why." |
| **Behind the build** | Authority | "How we built an autonomous quote agent for a Blaine fab shop" |

**Post Cadence:** 3–4 posts per week
**Format:** Short, specific, results-focused. No AI jargon.

### 3.4 Nextdoor

**Why:** Hyper-local, local business owners active, high trust environment

**Tactics:**
- Business page for Namakan AI
- Posts about local agent deployments (anonymized)
- "AI audit" offer as lead magnet
- Engage with local business conversations

### 3.5 Google Business Profile

**Setup:**
- Claim and optimize GBP for "Namakan AI Consulting"
- Categories: AI Consulting, Automation, Business Support Service
- Posts: Weekly, highlighting agent deployments
- Reviews: Ask satisfied clients to leave a review

### 3.6 Cold Outreach

**Email Sequences:**
- 5-email drip campaign (see email-outreach.md)
- LinkedIn connection + follow-up sequence
- Segment by industry, personalize with specific task examples

**Targeting:**
- 30–50 cold outreach per week initially
- Focus on manufacturing, healthcare, retail
- Lead with TASK COMPLETION, not "AI"

## 5. Lead Generation & Sales Process

### 5.1 Lead Magnets

| Lead Magnet | Description | Funnel Role |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Free AI Agent Audit** | 30-min call, we identify top 3 agent opportunities | Primary top-of-funnel |
| **AI Readiness Checklist** | 10-question self-assessment | Secondary, lower commitment |
| **Agent Demo** | Live demo for specific industry | Mid-funnel, pre-proposal |

### 5.2 Sales Process

1. **Discovery Call** (30 min, free) — Understand their business, identify task pain points
2. **AI Audit** (1 hr, $500 applied to project) — Deep dive on workflows, identify agent opportunities
3. **Proposal** (within 48 hrs) — Custom agent design + fixed pricing
4. **Contract** — 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
5. **Implementation** (2–4 weeks) — Build, test, train, launch
6. **Monthly Optimization** — Available retainer for ongoing agent management

### 5.3 Key Messages for Sales

**The Pitch:**
> "We build autonomous AI agents for local businesses — AI workers that complete tasks 24/7. Not chatbots. Not automation tools. Agents that DO."

**What we DON'T say:**
- "AI chatbots"
- "Workflow automation" (as a standalone product)
- "AI assistant" (implies human-in-the-loop)

**What we SAY instead:**
- Autonomous AI Agent
- AI Worker / AI Employee
- Your AI Workforce
- 24/7 AI employees
