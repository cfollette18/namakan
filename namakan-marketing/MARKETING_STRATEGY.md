| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|

| Budget authority | 20% | $500+/mo for the right solution |

**Secondary Segment: Mid-size businesses in Twin Cities metro**
- 50–200 employees
- More complex workflows = larger agent deployments

### 2.2 Industry Vertical Priority

**Primary Vertical: Manufacturing**
- High repetitive task volume (scheduling, inventory, quotes, follow-ups)
- 15+ hours/week typically automatable
- Receptive to efficiency gains
- **Target:** 30% of early customers

**Secondary Vertical: Healthcare / Medical**
- Front desk overload, appointment management, patient follow-ups
- 28%+ no-show rates are common and costly
- Strong ROI case for custom AI systems on proprietary data
- **Target:** 25% of early customers

**Tertiary Verticals:**
- Retail (20%) — Returns, order tracking, follow-ups
- Restaurants (15%) — Reservations, takeout orders, reviews
- Professional Services (10%) — Scheduling, document processing

### 2.3 Geographic Focus

**Priority Territory:**

| Priority | Region | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | **Blaine / Twin Cities** | Home base, local credibility, first-mover advantage |
| 2 | **Nationwide remote** | Any industry with proprietary data — healthcare, legal, manufacturing, finance |

## 3. Marketing Channels

### 3.1 Referral Marketing (Primary)

**Why it works first:**
- Local businesses trust peer recommendations
- Agent results are tangible and visible
- Referral fee creates incentive

**Referral Program:**
- 20% of first month's revenue for any referred client
- Formalize with a simple referral agreement
- Target: CPAs, web agencies, IT firms, other local business advisors

**Tactics:**
1. Ask every client at delivery: "Do you know another business owner who'd benefit from this?"
2. Formal referral program with written agreement
3. Annual review meeting = natural referral opportunity

### 3.2 Local Networking

**Priority Channels:**

| Channel | Why | Frequency |
|---------|-----|----------|
| Chamber of Commerce events | Local credibility, peer referrals | Monthly |
| BNI chapters | Structured referral networking | Weekly |
| Industry-specific meetups | Manufacturing, healthcare events | Monthly |
| SCORE / SBDC workshops | Establish authority, warm leads | Quarterly |

### 3.3 LinkedIn Content

**Content Pillars:**

| Pillar | Goal | Examples |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Agent completion stories** | Show what agents DO | "Here's how our agent processed 47 returns while the owner slept" |
| **Custom AI wins** | Social proof | Case studies from businesses with proprietary data |
| **AI vs. agents** | Differentiation | "We don't build chatbots. Here's why." |
| **Behind the build** | Authority | "How we built a fine-tuned model for a [manufacturing/healthcare/legal] firm" |

**Post Cadence:** 3–4 posts per week
**Format:** Short, specific, results-focused. No AI jargon.

### 3.4 Nextdoor

**Why:** Hyper-local, local business owners active, high trust environment

**Tactics:**
- Business page for Namakan AI
- Posts about local agent deployments (anonymized)
- "AI audit" offer as lead magnet
- Engage with local business conversations

### 3.5 Google Business Profile

**Setup:**
- Claim and optimize GBP for "Namakan AI Engineering"
- Categories: AI Consulting, Automation, Business Support Service
- Posts: Weekly, highlighting agent deployments
- Reviews: Ask satisfied clients to leave a review

### 3.6 Cold Outreach

**Email Sequences:**
- 5-email drip campaign (see email-outreach.md)
- LinkedIn connection + follow-up sequence
- Segment by industry, personalize with specific task examples

**Targeting:**
- 30–50 cold outreach per week initially
- Focus on manufacturing, healthcare, retail
- Lead with TASK COMPLETION, not "AI"

## 5. Lead Generation & Sales Process

### 5.1 Lead Magnets

| Lead Magnet | Description | Funnel Role |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Free AI Agent Audit** | 30-min call, we identify top 3 agent opportunities | Primary top-of-funnel |
| **AI Readiness Checklist** | 10-question self-assessment | Secondary, lower commitment |
| **Agent Demo** | Live demo for specific industry | Mid-funnel, pre-proposal |

### 5.2 Sales Process

1. **Discovery Call** (30 min, free) — Understand their business, identify task pain points
2. **AI Audit** (1 hr, $500 applied to project) — Deep dive on workflows, identify agent opportunities
3. **Proposal** (within 48 hrs) — Custom agent design + fixed pricing
4. **Contract** — 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
5. **Implementation** (2–4 weeks) — Build, test, train, launch
6. **Monthly Optimization** — Available retainer for ongoing agent management

### 5.3 Key Messages for Sales

**The Pitch:**
> "We build custom AI systems for businesses with proprietary data — fine-tuned models, RAG pipelines, and agentic workflows trained on YOUR data, integrated into YOUR systems. Not pre-built agents. Not generic tools. AI built for you."

**What we DON'T say:**
- "Pre-built agents"
- "Off-the-shelf AI"
- "AI tools" (as a standalone product)
- "Configure our platform"

**What we SAY instead:**
- Custom AI Engineering
- Fine-tuned models
- RAG pipelines built on your data
- AI trained on YOUR data
