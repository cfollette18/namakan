## 3. Ad Creative Specifications

### 3A. Static Image Specs

**Format A: Problem/Solution Split**
```
Left half: Dark/muted — Generic AI giving wrong answer, data silos visual
Right half: Bright/teal — Namakan custom AI giving right answer, unified data
Bottom: Namakan logo + "namakanai.com"
```
- **Dimensions:** 1200×628 (Feed), 1080×1080 (Square), 1080×1920 (Story)
- **Colors:** Teal (#0D9488), dark navy, white text
- **Fonts:** Bold sans-serif (Inter or Satoshi)
- **CTA overlay:** "Book Free Assessment" pill button

**Format B: Data-to-AI Transformation**
```
Visual: Data streams (documents, databases) flowing into a glowing neural network
Overlay text: "Your Data → Custom AI"
Bottom: Namakan logo + tagline
```
- **Dimensions:** Same as above
- **Mood:** Clean, technical, premium

**Format C: Team/Founder Trust Card**
```
Photo: Namakan team or Blaine/MN office aesthetic
Overlay: "Custom AI Engineering — Not a Config, a Build"
CTA: "Talk to Us"
```
- **Use:** Bottom-of-funnel, trust-building

### 3B. Video Hook Specs

**Hook Option 1: "The Generic AI Test" (0–3 sec)**
- Founder/operator shows phone screen: ChatGPT giving a useless generic answer
- Cut to text: "Generic AI doesn't know YOUR business."
- Style: Authentic, on-site, no polish needed — this is a strength

**Hook Option 2: "The Data Problem" (0–3 sec)**
- Montage of unused data: filing cabinets, spreadsheets, dusty binders
- Text: "15 years of proprietary data. Never used by AI."
- Style: Cinematic, moody lighting, relatable frustration

**Hook Option 3: "The AI Employee" (0–3 sec)**
- Split screen: Human employee at desk → AI agent completing the same task overnight
- Text: "While you sleep, your AI employee works."
- Style: Clean, professional, confident

**Video Format Requirements:**
| Platform | Format | Length | Spec |
|----------|--------|--------|------|
| Facebook Reels | Vertical 9:16 | 15–30s | 1080×1920, H.264, ≤ 30fps |
| Instagram Reels | Vertical 9:16 | 15–30s | 1080×1920, H.264 |
| Instagram Stories | Vertical 9:16 | 0–15s per segment | 1080×1920 |
| Facebook Feed | Square 1:1 or 16:9 | 15–60s | 1080×1080 or 1080×608 |

## 5. A/B Testing Recommendations

### Test 1: Video Hook Framing
**Hypothesis:** Problem-frustration hooks outperform aspirational hooks for cold audiences.
| Variant | Approach | Metric |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **A (Control)** | "Generic AI doesn't know YOUR business." — Frustration-led | Watch time, 3-sec retention |
| **B** | "Custom AI built for YOUR business." — Aspirational | Same |

**Duration:** 14 days or 500+ impressions minimum

---

### Test 2: CTA Button Copy
**Hypothesis:** "Book Free Assessment" outperforms generic "Learn More" for lead quality.
