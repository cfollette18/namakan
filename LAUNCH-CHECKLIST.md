## 1. LLC Filing Status

**Status: NOT YET FILED** ⚠️

The LLC has not yet been formally registered with the Minnesota Secretary of State.

### Action Required:
1. Go to [MN Secretary of State Business Portal](https://mblsportal.sos.mn.us/)
2. Search for "Namakan AI Consulting" to confirm name availability
3. File **Articles of Organization** (Minnesota Statutes Chapter 322B)
   - Fee: $100 (online) / $110 (paper)
   - Processing: 3–5 business days online
4. Designate a **Registered Agent** with a Minnesota physical address
5. Obtain **EIN** from IRS at [IRS.gov](https://www.irs.gov/entities/basic-requirements-for-llcs) (free, immediate)

## 4. Insurance

**Status: NOT OBTAINED** ⚠️

For AI consulting, the most important coverage is **Professional Liability (E&O)** + **Cyber Liability**.

### Recommended Coverage:
| Type | Coverage | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| General Liability | $1M per occurrence | Standard; covers client injuries, property damage |
| Professional Liability / E&O | $1M per occurrence | Covers negligence, errors in your advice/deliverables |
| Cyber Liability | $500K–$1M | Covers data breaches, AI system failures |

### Providers to Check:
- **Hiscox** (specializes in professional liability, online quotes)
- **Thimble** (short-term/ondemand policies)
- **Next Insurance** (fast online quote)
- **Local independent agent** (can bundle all three)

### Action Required:
- [ ] Get quotes from 2–3 providers
- [ ] Bind General Liability + Professional Liability (E&O) + Cyber Liability
- [ ] Request Certificate of Insurance (COI) to provide to clients
