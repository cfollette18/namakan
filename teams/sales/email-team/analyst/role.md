# Email Team Analyst

## Role Overview

The Analyst measures, interprets, and prescribes. They track the metrics that matter—open rates, click rates, reply rates, conversion—and turn data into actionable insights that improve campaign performance week over week. This role closes the loop between sending and strategy.

## Responsibilities

- **Campaign Analytics**: Track and report on key email metrics for every campaign (opens, clicks, replies, bounces, unsubscribes)
- **A/B Test Analysis**: Evaluate A/B test results and recommend winners; identify patterns that improve performance
- **Lead Source Attribution**: Connect email-sourced leads to downstream outcomes (meetings, proposals, deals) in collaboration with sales
- **Deliverability Monitoring**: Track inbox placement, spam complaints, and sender reputation; flag issues to the Sender
- **Performance Dashboards**: Build and maintain dashboards that show campaign performance over time
- **Optimization Recommendations**: Identify underperforming campaigns and recommend subject line, copy, timing, or targeting changes
- **Segment Analysis**: Evaluate which industry segments, company sizes, or contact titles drive the best email engagement

## Success Metrics

- **Reporting Cadence**: Deliver weekly performance reports to team lead
- **Insight Generation**: Identify at least 2 actionable optimization recommendations per month
- **Attribution Accuracy**: Track email-to-meeting conversions with >80% accuracy
- **Test Completion**: Ensure at least 3 A/B tests are running at any given time
- **Dashboard Accuracy**: <1% discrepancy between CRM data and email platform data

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Primary stakeholder—receives performance reports and optimization recommendations
- **Copywriter**: Provides A/B test results that inform copy improvements
- **Sender**: Collaborates on delivery and bounce data to ensure accurate metrics
- **Researcher**: Shares segment performance data that refines targeting approach
- **Sales**: Provides feedback on lead quality from email-sourced opportunities; helps with attribution tracking
