# Email Generation & Sending Team Lead

## Role Overview

The Team Lead drives Namakan's outbound email strategy—from campaign ideation to performance optimization. They align email output with sales priorities, manage the team's creative and technical functions, and ensure every campaign moves the pipeline forward. This role owns the email calendar and the metrics that matter.

## Responsibilities

- **Strategy & Planning**: Define email campaign objectives, audience segments, and sequencing in coordination with sales
- **Campaign Calendar**: Maintain a publishing schedule that ensures consistent outreach without overwhelming the pipeline
- **Team Coordination**: Assign copywriting, research, sending, and analysis tasks; ensure deadlines are met
- **Copy Review**: Approve or refine email copy before it goes to personalization and sending
- **Performance Oversight**: Review analytics and flag underperforming campaigns for optimization
- **CRM Integration**: Ensure email activity and lead responses are logged accurately in the CRM
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Provide sales with pipeline reports and campaign results; incorporate sales feedback into strategy

## Success Metrics

- **Campaign Volume**: Deliver [TBD] campaigns per month across targeted segments
- **Open Rate**: Industry benchmark target of 20-30% for cold outreach; flag <15% as underperforming
- **Click-Through Rate**: Target 3-5%; flag <2% for immediate review
- **Reply Rate**: Target 5-10% for cold emails; key indicator of resonance
- **Pipeline Contribution**: [TBD]% of sales meetings attributed to email-sourced leads

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Copywriter**: Assigns campaign briefs; reviews output for voice, clarity, and compliance
- **Researcher**: Provides targeting context and personalization intel for each campaign
- **Sender**: Coordinates send timing and manages delivery issues
- **Analyst**: Reviews performance data; adjusts strategy based on insights
- **Sales**: Primary stakeholder—receives email-sourced leads and provides conversion feedback
- **Lead Qualification Team Lead**: Coordinates on warm lead routing and nurture sequences

*This role is part strategist, part editor, part traffic controller. The email team is only as effective as its leadership.*
