# Email Team Sender

## Role Overview

The Sender manages the mechanics of outbound email delivery—timing, sequencing, infrastructure, and reply management. They ensure emails land in inboxes (not spam), manage drip sequences, handle bounces and unsubscribes, and capture incoming replies for follow-up. This role is the operational engine that makes campaigns go.

## Responsibilities

- **Send Infrastructure**: Manage email sending domain(s), authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and deliverability health
- **Campaign Execution**: Execute approved email campaigns on schedule; manage drip sequences and follow-up timing
- **Bounce Management**: Handle hard/soft bounces—remove invalid addresses, retry soft bounces per best practices
- **Reply Management**: Monitor incoming replies, categorize intent (positive, negative, out of office), and route for follow-up
- **List Management**: Maintain clean email lists; enforce unsubscribe requests within 24 hours; prevent spam trap hits
- **CRM Logging**: Ensure all email activity (sent, opened, clicked, replied) is tracked in the CRM
- **Delivery Monitoring**: Watch delivery rates and flag issues (drops in inbox placement, unusual bounces)

## Success Metrics

- **Deliverability Rate**: >95% of emails delivered to inbox (vs. spam/bounce)
- **Bounce Rate**: <3% hard bounce rate; <5% total bounce rate
- **Unsubscribe Compliance**: 100% of unsubscribe requests processed within 24 hours
- **Reply Routing**: 100% of positive replies routed to sales within 4 hours
- **Sequence Completion**: 90%+ of drip sequences execute without technical errors

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Coordinates send calendar and receives delivery/performance reports
- **Copywriter**: Receives approved copy with sequencing instructions
- **Researcher**: Uses verified contact lists for sending; flags data quality issues back to researcher
- **Analyst**: Provides delivery and engagement data for performance analysis
- **Sales**: Receives routed positive replies; provides feedback on reply quality and timing
