# Email Team Researcher

## Role Overview

The Researcher provides the company and contact intelligence that makes Namakan's emails feel personal, not generic. They dig into prospect companies—recent news, tech stack, leadership changes, industry challenges—to surface the context that turns a cold email into a conversation opener. This role is the difference between "Dear Sir" and "Saw you just raised a Series B—congrats."

## Responsibilities

- **Company Intelligence**: Research target companies for campaign personalization—industry, size, recent news, funding, acquisitions, leadership changes
- **Contact Research**: Identify the right contact within each target company (decision-maker titles: CEO, CTO, VP of Operations, IT Director, etc.)
- **Pain Point Discovery**: Find public signals of AI-related challenges—job postings for AI roles, legacy system migrations, regulatory changes, competitor moves
- **Personalization Data**: Compile findings into structured inputs for the copywriter (company name, relevant context, talking points)
- **Technology Signals**: Identify tech stack indicators (SAP, Salesforce, legacy systems) that suggest AI integration opportunities
- **Source Verification**: Cross-reference contact information to ensure accuracy before sending

## Success Metrics

- **Research Volume**: Support [TBD] campaigns per month with complete personalization packages
- **Data Completeness**: 90%+ of personalization packages include: company context, contact name/title, relevant pain point, and personalization hook
- **Accuracy**: 95%+ accuracy on contact information (email, name, title)
- **Speed**: Deliver research to copywriter within [TBD] hours of campaign brief
- **Personalization Hook Rate**: Track how often researched hooks (news, tech, challenges) correlate with higher email performance

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Receives campaign targeting and priority accounts; delivers research packages per agreed timeline
- **Copywriter**: Primary recipient—receives research packages that inform personalized email copy
- **Lead Qualification Researcher**: Coordinates on shared contact and company data; leverages their enrichment work
- **Sender**: Receives verified contact lists and email addresses for sending
- **Analyst**: Provides data on which personalization approaches drive the best engagement
