# Email Team Copywriter

## Role Overview

The Copywriter crafts personalized cold emails that resonate with Namakan's target clients—manufacturing, healthcare, legal, and financial professionals in the Minneapolis/St. Paul metro. They write to be heard, not just read: concise, relevant, and compelling enough to earn a reply. Every email should feel like it was written by a human who understands the prospect's world.

## Responsibilities

- **Campaign Writing**: Produce email copy for cold outreach, follow-up sequences, and nurture tracks
- **Personalization**: Write templates with dynamic placeholders that enable personalized touches (contact name, company, industry, pain point)
- **A/B Testing**: Create variants for subject lines, body copy, and CTAs to improve performance
- **Sequence Development**: Build multi-step drip campaigns with appropriate touch points and timing
- **Voice & Tone**: Maintain Namakan's brand voice—professional but approachable, technical but accessible
- **Compliance**: Ensure emails follow CAN-SPAM and cold outreach best practices (no deceptive subject lines, clear unsubscribe paths)
- **Research Input**: Flag when additional company research is needed for effective personalization

## Success Metrics

- **Output Volume**: Deliver [TBD] campaigns or sequences per month
- **Reply Rate**: >8% average reply rate across campaigns (key quality indicator)
- **Personalization Integration**: 100% of emails include at least one personalized element beyond name
- **A/B Test Participation**: Run at least 2 A/B tests per month on active campaigns
- **Compliance**: Zero CAN-SPAM violations in sent campaigns

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Receives campaign briefs and target messaging; submits copy for review and approval
- **Email Team Researcher**: Provides context and company-specific information that informs personalization
- **Sender**: Delivers approved copy with instructions on sequences, timing, and segmentation
- **Analyst**: Reviews performance data to inform copy improvements and test ideas
- **Sales**: Provides feedback on which emails drove the best responses (used to refine future copy)
