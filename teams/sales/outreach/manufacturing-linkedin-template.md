## Tips for Using These Messages

1. **Personalize** — Add something specific you notice about their company
2. **Connect first** — Don't pitch immediately
3. **Follow up** — Wait 3-5 days after connecting before sending follow-up
4. **Provide value** — Share insights, not just pitches
