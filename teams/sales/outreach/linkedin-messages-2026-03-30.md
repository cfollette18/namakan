## How to Use These Messages

1. **Copy the connection request** — paste into LinkedIn connection request
2. **Wait for acceptance** — usually 1-3 days
3. **Send the follow up message** — creates conversation

### Tips:
- Keep it short — LinkedIn cuts off long messages
- Personalize slightly if you see something specific about them
- Connect first, pitch later
- Don't pitch immediately — build rapport first

### Priority Order:
1. Law firms (lawyers respond well to LinkedIn)
2. Construction firms (VP/President level)
3. Regional banks (CEO/President)
4. Healthcare systems (VP level)
5. Manufacturing (President/VP level)
