# Lead Qualification Escalator

## Role Overview

The Escalator manages high-priority leads that are ready for immediate sales engagement. They ensure hot leads don't lose momentum during handoff—preparing the brief, coordinating with sales, and confirming receipt. Speed and accuracy are critical: the best lead is worthless if it arrives too late or incomplete.

## Responsibilities

- **Hot Lead Intake**: Receive qualified leads from the Qualifier with full context (company, contact, pain points, budget, timeline)
- **Brief Preparation**: Compile a concise sales-ready summary including company background, identified needs, qualification history, and recommended approach
- **Sales Coordination**: Deliver the brief to the appropriate sales rep; confirm bandwidth and readiness to engage
- **Follow-Up Tracking**: Ensure sales responds within 24 hours; escalate delays to team lead
- **Feedback Loop**: Collect sales outcome data (meeting held, proposal submitted, deal closed, or lost) and log for qualification refinement
- **Urgency Monitoring**: Track leads that need immediate attention (active procurement, time-sensitive budget, competitive situation)
- **CRM Updates**: Maintain accurate status on all escalated leads through outcome resolution

## Success Metrics

- **Escalation Speed**: Deliver hot leads to sales within 4 hours of qualification
- **Brief Quality**: >95% of briefs contain sufficient context for sales to engage without additional research
- **Sales Response Rate**: 90%+ of escalated leads receive sales outreach within 24 hours
- **Pipeline Visibility**: 100% of escalated leads tracked in CRM from escalation through outcome
- **Feedback Capture**: 80%+ of escalated leads have outcome data logged within 2 weeks of handoff

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Escalates complex or high-value leads for priority attention; reports escalation outcomes
- **Qualifier**: Receives qualified hot leads; provides full context to avoid re-qualification by sales
- **Sales**: Primary recipient—receives prepared briefs and provides outcome feedback
- **Email Team**: If an escalated lead isn't ready for direct sales (e.g., needs more nurture), routes back for Email Team engagement before re-escalation
- **Analyst**: Provides data on escalation success rates to identify qualification improvements

---

*The escalator is the bridge between autonomous lead qualification and human sales execution. They make the handoff frictionless.*
