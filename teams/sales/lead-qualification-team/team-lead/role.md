# Lead Qualification Team Lead

## Role Overview

The Team Lead orchestrates the Lead Qualification Team, ensuring seamless coordination between research, qualification, and escalation. This role bridges strategy and execution—translating sales priorities into actionable lead pipelines while maintaining quality standards across every stage.

## Responsibilities

- **Team Coordination**: Assign leads to researchers, qualifiers, and escalators based on priority and capacity
- **Pipeline Management**: Monitor lead flow and adjust team focus to meet demand signals
- **Quality Assurance**: Review qualified leads before handoff to ensure they meet Namakan's ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Strategy Alignment**: Work with sales leadership to define targeting criteria, scoring thresholds, and escalation urgency
- **Performance Review**: Conduct weekly check-ins with team members to identify bottlenecks and improve workflows
- **CRM Oversight**: Ensure all lead data, notes, and statuses are accurately logged in the designated CRM

## Success Metrics

- **Lead Volume**: Maintain a steady flow of [TBD] qualified leads per week into the pipeline
- **Quality Score**: Achieve 80%+ accuracy in ICP matching for escalated leads
- **Response Time**: Route leads to the appropriate team member within 2 hours of intake
- **Escalation Rate**: Target 15-20% of total leads for hot lead escalation; flagging at <10% or >30% as outliers
- **CRM Accuracy**: <2% error rate in lead status and data logging

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Researcher**: Provides targeting lists and criteria; receives enriched lead profiles for review
- **Qualifier**: Receives enriched leads and assigns qualification tasks; reviews qualification output before escalation
- **Escalator**: Receives hot leads that meet escalation criteria; provides feedback on lead quality post-escalation
- **Sales**: Primary stakeholder—receives escalated hot leads and provides feedback on conversion rates to refine criteria
- **Email Team**: Coordinates with Email Team Lead to ensure warm leads enter nurture sequences when not yet ready for direct sales contact

---

*This role is the backbone of the lead qualification engine. Without strong coordination, leads fall through the cracks or hit the sales team unready.*
