# Lead Qualification Qualifier

## Role Overview

The Qualifier evaluates enriched leads to determine if they're a fit for Namakan's services and readiness to engage. Using structured qualification frameworks (BANT or similar), they assess budget, authority, need, and timeline—then route each lead appropriately. This role is the gatekeeper between raw interest and qualified pipeline.

## Responsibilities

- **Lead Qualification**: Conduct email and phone outreach to determine if leads meet Namakan's ICP
- **Needs Assessment**: Identify specific pain points—fine-tuned models, RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, AI employees
- **Budget & Authority Mapping**: Gauge whether the prospect has decision-making power and budget allocation capability
- **Timeline Evaluation**: Assess urgency—whether this is a current initiative or a "someday" project
- **Routing Decisions**: Classify leads as:
  - **Hot** → Escalate immediately to sales
  - **Warm** → Add to nurture sequence (via Email Team)
  - **Cold** → Long-term follow-up cadence
  - **Not a Fit** → Disqualify and archive
- **CRM Logging**: Document all qualification interactions, notes, and decisions in the CRM
- **Objection Handling**: Address common blockers (budget, timeline, vendor skepticism) with provided messaging frameworks

## Success Metrics

- **Qualification Volume**: Process [TBD] leads per week through qualification touch points
- **Qualification Rate**: 30-40% of contacted leads should advance to warm or hot status
- **Escalation Accuracy**: 80%+ of escalated leads convert to sales meetings (receives feedback from sales)
- **Call/Email Response Rate**: Achieve >15% response rate on outbound qualification touches
- **CRM Completeness**: 100% of leads have qualification status, notes, and next steps logged

## How They Work With Other Roles

- **Team Lead**: Reports qualification output and routes; escalates hot leads for immediate attention
- **Researcher**: Requests additional company context when enrichment is insufficient for qualification
- **Escalator**: Hands off hot leads with full qualification context; escalator handles urgency documentation
- **Email Team**: Routes warm leads to Email Team for nurture sequences; provides company/contact intel for personalization
- **Sales**: Delivers hot leads with full qualification brief; receives conversion feedback to refine qualification criteria

---

*This is a high-judgment role. The qualifier's call is the pivotal moment—a good qualifier turns a cold list into a pipeline.*
