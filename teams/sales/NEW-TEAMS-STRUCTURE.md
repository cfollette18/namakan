# New Sales Teams Structure

## Overview

Two new specialized AI agent teams form the front end of Namakan's sales pipeline: the **Lead Qualification Team** and the **Email Generation & Sending Team**. Together they create a continuous flow of qualified opportunities—from cold prospect to sales-ready lead—while scaling outreach beyond what human-only sales can achieve.

---

## Why These Teams Are Needed

### The Problem

Traditional sales outreach has a ceiling. Human SDRs can make 50-100 calls/day. Cold emails go out at low volume with generic messaging. Lead qualification is inconsistent, and hot leads sit waiting while reps are busy elsewhere.

### The Solution

These two teams operate in tandem to:
1. **Identify** the right prospects (companies matching Namakan's ICP)
2. **Enrich** them with context and contact data
3. **Qualify** them for readiness and fit
4. **Escalate** hot leads to sales immediately
5. **Nurture** warm leads with personalized email campaigns
6. **Track** every touchpoint and outcome for continuous improvement

### The Outcome

A pipeline that self-organizes by priority. Sales gets leads that are already vetted, contextualized, and ready for conversation. Marketing gets scalable outreach that doesn't feel mass-produced.

## How Lead Qualification Feeds the Email Team

```
Lead Qualification Team          Email Team
─────────────────────────        ───────────
Researcher → Enriches leads
                      ↓
Qualifier → Routes WARM leads ───→ Email Team Researcher → Personalization
                      ↓                     ↓
                  (not yet hot)    Copywriter → Craft email
                                       ↓
                                   Sender → Execute drip sequence
                                       ↓
                                   Analyst → Track & optimize
                                       ↓
                          (re-engage until hot)
                                   Escalator ← (if reply rate indicates hot lead)
```

**The Handoff**: When a lead is warm (good fit, but not ready to buy now), the Qualifier routes it to the Email Team with full context. The Email Team Researcher uses that context to personalize a nurture sequence. The lead receives targeted emails over weeks or months—building familiarity with Namakan until they're ready to engage. If engagement signals spike (reply, click, website visit), the Escalator pulls them back into the qualification flow for immediate sales handoff.

## How the Email Team Feeds Sales (Human)

```
Email Outreach          Sales
──────────────         ─────
Campaign runs →   Replies/Responses → Route positive replies → Sales rep engages
                                    → Log in CRM with email context

Ongoing nurture → Intent signals → Escalator re-qualifies → Sales receives warm lead

Reporting → Pipeline contribution → Sales confirms meeting/demo → Attribution closes loop
```

**The Handoff**: Email replies with buying signals (positive intent, request for demo, questions about pricing) are routed to sales within 4 hours. The sender captures and categorizes replies; the escalator prepares a brief with email context and routes it directly to the appropriate sales rep.

**Attribution**: The analyst tracks which email campaigns and sequences generate sales meetings. This closes the loop—sales knows exactly which emails worked, and the email team knows what resonates.

## Priority Hiring Order

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Hire These First)

1. **Lead Qualification Researcher** — Without research, nothing else has data. This role builds the pipeline from scratch.
2. **Lead Qualification Qualifier** — The gatekeeper. Must have strong judgment and CRM skills.
3. **Email Team Sender** — Infrastructure role. Get this right before sending at scale.
4. **Email Team Copywriter** — Need copy to execute campaigns. Strong writing for technical B2B context.

**Rationale**: These four roles can operate at minimum viable scale. Research → Qualify → Send → Write. The loop can run.

### Phase 2 — Amplification (Hire After Foundation)

5. **Lead Qualification Team Lead** — Once you have researchers and qualifiers, you need coordination.
6. **Email Team Researcher** — Personalization at scale requires dedicated research for email.
7. **Email Team Analyst** — Measure what's working. Optimize the loop.

**Rationale**: With Phase 1 running, add leadership and measurement. The team grows from functional to strategic.

### Phase 3 — Scale & Optimize (Final Hires)

8. **Email Team Team Lead** — Strategy, campaign planning, stakeholder management.
9. **Lead Qualification Escalator** — With volume up, hot leads need dedicated handoff management.

**Rationale**: By Phase 3, you're running multiple campaigns and have significant lead volume. Need dedicated escalation and email leadership.

## Notes

- All roles can be staffed with AI agents, human contractors, or hybrid depending on budget and scale needs

- Email sending infrastructure (domain, authentication, warming) should be established before Phase 2 scales
- Weekly sync between Lead Qualification Team Lead and Email Team Lead to align on warm lead routing
