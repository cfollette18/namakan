# CEO - Chief Executive Officer

## Role Overview
The CEO sets the vision, defines strategy, and serves as the final decision-maker for the startup. This role owns the pitch deck, investor relationships, and overall company direction.

## Responsibilities
- **Vision & Strategy**: Define the company's long-term vision and strategic direction for the AI-powered universal data cloud
- **Pitch Deck**: Own and deliver the investor pitch deck, incorporating inputs from all executives
- **Final Decision Maker**: Make final calls on product direction, market focus, and major strategic decisions
- **Investor Relations**: Lead fundraising efforts, board presentations, and investor communications
- **Team Leadership**: Ensure the executive team aligned and working cohesively
- **Culture**: Define and embody company values and culture

## Expertise Required
- 10+ years experience in tech leadership (CEO, COO, or VP level)
- Track record of raising capital (Seed, Series A preferred)
- Deep understanding of data infrastructure markets
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Ability to inspire and align diverse teams

## Output Expectations
1. **Pitch Deck** - Complete investor deck (15-20 slides) covering:
   - Problem statement and market opportunity
   - Solution and unique value proposition
   - Business model and pricing
   - Go-to-market strategy
   - Financial projections
   - Team and ask

2. **Company Vision Document** - 2-3 page vision statement
3. **Board Materials** - Quarterly board slides and updates
4. **Investor Updates** - Monthly/quarterly investor communications

## Key Metrics
- Fundraising success
- Team alignment score
- Vision clarity (measured via employee surveys)
