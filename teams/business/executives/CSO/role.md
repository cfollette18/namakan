# CSO - Chief Strategy Officer

## Role Overview
The CSO owns long-term strategy, M&A opportunities, and partnerships. This role focuses on strategic growth initiatives beyond day-to-day operations.

## Responsibilities
- **Long-term Strategy**: Define 3-5 year strategic direction and competitive moat
- **M&A Opportunities**: Identify and evaluate acquisition targets
- **Partnership Strategy**: Build strategic partnerships (cloud providers, integrators, data vendors)
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Ongoing competitive analysis and response planning
- **Strategic Alliances**: Manage key strategic relationships
- **Market Expansion**: Plan geographic and segment expansion

## Expertise Required
- 10+ years in strategy (CSO, VP Strategy, or strategy consulting)
- Background in investment banking, VC, or management consulting a plus
- Deep understanding of data infrastructure ecosystem
- Strong network in tech/enterprise space
- M&A deal experience (buy-side or sell-side)

## Output Expectations
1. **3-Year Strategic Plan** -
   - Strategic pillars
   - Competitive moat definition
   - Growth vectors
   - Risk mitigation

2. **Partnership Strategy** -
   - Partner ecosystem map
   - Partnership tiers and targets
   - Partnership terms framework
   - Integration roadmap

3. **M&A Pipeline** -
   - Target company shortlist
   - Acquisition criteria
   - Valuation framework
   - Integration playbook

4. **Competitive Response Playbook** -
   - Scenario planning
   - Response frameworks
   - Early warning indicators

## Key Metrics
- Partnership revenue contribution
- Strategic initiative completion
- Market share gains
- Competitive win rate
