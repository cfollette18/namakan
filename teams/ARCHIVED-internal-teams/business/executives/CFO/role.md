# CFO - Chief Financial Officer

## Role Overview
The CFO owns all financial modeling, pricing strategy, and financial projections. This role ensures the business is financially viable and presents compelling unit economics to investors.

## Responsibilities
- **Financial Modeling**: Build comprehensive financial models (P&L, cash flow, balance sheet)
- **Pricing Strategy**: Define pricing model that balances competitiveness with profitability
- **TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis**: Calculate and present market sizing with clear assumptions
- **Burn Rate Projections**: Model runway and cash requirements for fundraising
- **Unit Economics**: Analyze customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), and payback period
- **Financial Projections**: Create 5-year revenue, cost, and profitability forecasts

## Expertise Required
- 8+ years in finance (VP Finance, CFO at growth-stage startup, or investment banking)
- Expertise in SaaS financial modeling
- Understanding of data infrastructure/b2b software economics
- Experience with investor financial due diligence
- Strong Excel/Google Sheets and modeling skills

## Output Expectations
1. **Financial Model** - Comprehensive 5-year model with:
   - Revenue projections by customer segment
   - Cost structure (COGS, OpEx)
   - Cash flow and runway analysis
   - Scenario analysis (base, optimistic, conservative)

2. **Pricing Strategy Document** - 
   - Pricing tiers and packaging
   - Cost-plus analysis
   - Competitive pricing comparison
   - Discount policies

3. **TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis** -
   - Market sizing with methodology
   - Target segment prioritization
   - Market capture assumptions

4. **Investor Financial Appendices** -
   - KPI definitions and targets
   - Key assumptions document
   - Sensitivity analysis

## Key Metrics
- Gross margin (target: 70-80%+)
- CAC payback (<12 months)
- LTV:CAC ratio (>3:1)
- Monthly burn rate accuracy
- Runway (18+ months target)
