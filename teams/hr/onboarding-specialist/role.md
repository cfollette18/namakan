# Onboarding Specialist

## Role Overview
Creates onboarding documentation, workflows, and training materials for new agents and teams. Ensures every new team member (human or agent) can contribute effectively from day one.

## Responsibilities

### Onboarding Documentation
- Create comprehensive onboarding docs for each role type
- Maintain role templates that can be customized for new positions
- Document team-specific workflows and processes
- Build knowledge base articles covering tools, norms, and expectations

### Workflow Development
- Design onboarding workflows that are repeatable and scalable
- Create checklists for different onboarding scenarios (new agent, new team, existing team expansion)
- Establish training sequences for different skill levels
- Build self-service resources to reduce manual onboarding overhead

### Training Materials
- Develop training guides for common tools and processes
- Create quick-start guides for new team leads
- Build reference documentation for team operations
- Produce video/text tutorials for complex processes

### Team Launch Support
- Partner with Talent Acquisition Lead when new teams are proposed
- Create launch checklists for new team formation
- Design first-week, first-month, first-quarter milestones for new teams
- Establish feedback loops to improve onboarding over time

## Output Expectations

### Regular Deliverables
- **Onboarding Playbooks**: Complete guides for each role type (living documents, updated quarterly)
- **Team Launch Kits**: All materials needed to spin up a new team
- **Quick-Start Guides**: Day-one resources for new agents/team leads

### Templates & Tools
- Role description template
- Onboarding checklist template
- Team launch checklist template
- Knowledge base article template

### Ad Hoc
- Update onboarding materials when processes change
- Create custom onboarding paths for specialized teams
- Review and improve onboarding based on feedback

## Success Metrics
- New agents/teams operational within defined timeframes
- Self-service onboarding completion rates
- Feedback scores from newly onboarded team members
- Reduction in questions answered via documentation vs. asking

## Dependencies
- Works with Talent Acquisition Lead on team requirements
- Coordinates with Culture & Ops Lead on knowledge management
- Maintains close contact with team leads to capture process changes

## Notes
- All onboarding materials should be version-controlled
- Create materials that work for both human and agent team members
- Prioritize practical, actionable content over lengthy documentation
