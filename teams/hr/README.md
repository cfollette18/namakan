# HR/People Operations Team - Namakan

## Team Purpose
The HR/People Operations team manages the agentic workforce by continuously assessing capabilities, recommending new teams, creating onboarding workflows, and maintaining organizational knowledge.

## Team Structure

### 1. Talent Acquisition Lead
- **Focus**: Strategic workforce planning
- **Key outputs**: Team recommendations, skill gap analyses, workforce assessments
- **Drives**: New team formations, capability expansion

### 2. Onboarding Specialist
- **Focus**: New team member enablement
- **Key outputs**: Onboarding docs, team launch kits, training materials
- **Drives**: Fast time-to-productivity for new agents and teams

### 3. Culture & Ops Lead
- **Focus**: Team health and operations
- **Key outputs**: Communication protocols, knowledge base, team health reports
- **Drives**: Smooth operations, documentation, collaboration

## How They Work Together

```
Talent Acquisition Lead
        │
        ▼
   [Proposes new team]
        │
        ▼
Onboarding Specialist ◄── Creates launch materials
        │
        ▼
Culture & Ops Lead ───→ Manages ongoing operations
```

## Core Responsibilities

### Continuously Assess Capabilities
- Talent Acquisition Lead identifies gaps
- Culture & Ops Lead monitors team health/capacity
- Quarterly capability reviews

### Recommend New Teams
- Talent Acquisition Lead proposes
- Onboarding Specialist prepares launch plan
- Founder approves/rejects

### Create Onboarding Workflows
- Onboarding Specialist creates materials
- Culture & Ops Lead integrates into knowledge base
- All teams use for new additions

### Maintain Knowledge Base
- Culture & Ops Lead owns and curates
- All teams contribute updates
- Onboarding Specialist references for training

## Success Metrics
- Workforce plans align with company strategy
- New teams onboard efficiently (target: 1 week for docs, 2 weeks operational)
- Knowledge base is current and searchable
- Team health scores remain positive

## Cadence
- **Weekly**: HR sync (all three roles)
- **Monthly**: Workforce assessment, knowledge base review
- **Quarterly**: Strategic workforce planning with founder
- **Ad hoc**: Team launch proposals, incident response
