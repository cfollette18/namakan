## Why Colab?

**Cost and Simplicity:**
- No expensive GPU needed — Colab handles training
- Fast iteration — training takes 45 minutes
- Proven stack — Qwen2.5-8B is efficient and accurate

**Data Security:**
- PII is removed before training
- Only training outputs (model weights) are retained
- We sign NDAs before data access
- Your raw data never leaves your systems during cleaning

**Client:** Metal fabrication company, Minneapolis  
**Data:** Salesforce (tickets), PostgreSQL (orders/QC), Google Drive (SOPs)

**What happened:**
