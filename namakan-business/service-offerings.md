## 👤 Service 2: Custom Agents

**AI workers trained on your business, integrated into your systems.**

### What It Is

Custom agents are AI workers that autonomously complete multi-step tasks in your existing systems. They're not chatbots that respond — they're AI that acts. They process documents, update records, trigger actions, escalate when needed, and work 24/7.

Think of it as hiring a virtual employee that knows your processes, works in your systems, and handles the repetitive multi-step tasks that eat up your team's time.

### What You Get

- **Initial training** on your business domain, processes, and standards
- **Ongoing refinement** as the agent learns from real interactions
- **System integrations** — connected to your CRM, documents, databases, and workflows
- **Proactive operation** — the agent doesn't just answer questions, it takes initiative
- **Clear escalation paths** — the agent knows when to bring in a human
- **Monthly performance reviews** — we track what the agent is doing and optimize continuously
- **Full ownership** — trained on YOUR data, for YOUR business

### Example Roles

| Agent Role | What It Does |
|------------|--------------|
| **Client Onboarding Agent** | Manages full client onboarding — document collection, compliance checks, account setup, first-meeting prep |
| **Contract Review Agent** | Reviews contracts for standard terms, flags deviations, prepares summary for attorney review |
| **Sales Development Agent** | Qualifies inbound leads, schedules appointments, drafts personalized follow-up sequences |
| **Operations Coordinator Agent** | Monitors inventory, manages purchase orders, coordinates with suppliers, alerts management |
| **Research Analyst Agent** | Monitors your industry, competitors, and market — delivers daily briefs and flags opportunities |
