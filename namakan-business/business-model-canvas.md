## Summary

| Block | Details |
|---|---|
| **Who we serve** | Blaine + north metro SMBs: manufacturing, healthcare, retail, general small business |
| **What we offer** | Autonomous AI agents ($500–2K/mo), multi-agent deployments ($2K–5K), custom AI workforce projects ($5K+) |
| **How we reach them** | Local SEO, Nextdoor, networking, referrals |
| **How we relate** | Consultative, hands-on, local, no-jargon |
| **Revenue model** | One-time project fees + monthly maintenance/retainer |
| **Key costs** | Tool subscriptions, networking, minimal marketing |
| **Key differentiator** | Local, affordable, fast, plain-English AI for Main Street |
