# Namakan AI Engineering — Custom AI Engineering Outreach

>
> **Sales Cycle:** 4–12 weeks from first contact to signed contract. This sequence reflects that patience. We're building relationships, not pushing product.

> **Rule:** Research before every send. Insert specifics. Generic emails to this tier of client will destroy trust.

## 🎯 Segment Variations

### For Healthcare/Medical:
- Lead with patient outcomes and clinical workflow, not efficiency
- Reference HIPAA and data security as a given, not a selling point
- Emphasize RAG on clinical notes, fine-tuning on patient population data
- Example framing: "AI that understands YOUR patient population, not just general medical knowledge"

### For Manufacturing:
- Lead with quality, yield, and operational efficiency
- Reference specific workflows: inspection, scheduling, predictive maintenance
- Emphasize fine-tuning on proprietary process data
- Example framing: "AI that learned from YOUR machines, YOUR operators, YOUR defect history"

### For Financial Services:
- Lead with risk, compliance, and decision support
- Reference proprietary models and actuarial data
- Emphasize audit trails and explainability
- Example framing: "AI that understands YOUR risk models, YOUR book of business, YOUR regulatory context"

### For Legal:
- Lead with research efficiency and precedent discovery
- Reference proprietary case outcomes and deal history
- Emphasize accuracy and source citation
- Example framing: "AI that has read YOUR cases, YOUR deals, YOUR clients — and knows how you think"
