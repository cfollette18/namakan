# Lead Qualification Checklist

**Namakan AI Engineering | Custom AI Systems**

---

## The 4 Key Areas

| Area | Question | Ideal Answer |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Budget** | Can they afford $5K-15K upfront or $2K/mo retainer? | Yes |
| **Authority** | Are they the decision maker? | Yes |
| **Timeline** | Do they need this soon? | 30-60 days |
| **Need** | Do they have a real problem? | Yes |

---

## Budget Qualification

### Required Questions

- [ ] "What's your monthly budget for a solution like this?"
- [ ] "Have you allocated any funds for AI or automation this quarter?"
- [ ] "What did you last spend on [relevant software/contractor]?"

### Budget Ranges

| Budget | Fit |
|--------|-----|
| $3K upfront | ✅ Sprint / Discovery |
| $5K-15K | ✅ Build |
| $2K/mo | ✅ Retainer |
| $25K+ | ✅ Enterprise |
| <$500/mo | ❌ May need smaller start |
| "Free" | ❌ Not a fit |

---

## Authority Qualification

### Required Questions

- [ ] "Are you the decision maker for this?"
- [ ] "Who else would be involved in choosing a solution like this?"
- [ ] "What's your process for bringing on a new vendor?"

### Authority Levels

| Level | What It Means | Next Step |
|-------|---------------|-----------|
| Decision Maker | Can say yes today | Go to close |
| Influencer | Recommends, can't buy | Build relationship |
| Gatekeeper | Controls access | Find DM |
| Blocked | No budget/authority | Nurture only |

---

## Timeline Qualification

### Required Questions

- [ ] "When are you looking to have this implemented?"
- [ ] "What's driving that timeline?"
- [ ] "What happens if you don't solve this by [their date]?"

### Timeline Fit

| Timeline | Fit | Action |
|----------|-----|--------|
| ASAP (1-2 weeks) | ✅ Hot lead | Priority call |
| 30 days | ✅ Good fit | Standard flow |
| 60-90 days | ⚠️ Long cycle | Nurture campaign |
| "Someday" | ❌ Not a fit | Keep warm |
| No timeline | ❌ No urgency | Educate |

---

## Need Qualification

### Required Questions

- [ ] "What's the biggest pain point you're trying to solve?"
- [ ] "How many hours/week does your team spend on [problem area]?"
- [ ] "What have you tried before to solve this?"
- [ ] "What would this be worth to you?"

### Need Severity

| Severity | Indicator | Action |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Critical | Losing money, major pain | Immediate fit |
| High | Significant time drain | Strong fit |
| Medium | Annoying but manageable | Good fit |
| Low | "Nice to have" | Keep warm |

---

## Red Flags ❌

- [ ] No budget or authority
- [ ] No clear timeline
- [ ] "We tried everything"
- [ ] Just researching, not buying
- [ ] Wants custom AI built from scratch (too big)
- [ ] Expects magic overnight
- [ ] Only wants off-the-shelf AI (not custom-built)

---

## Green Flags ✅

- [ ] Clear pain point
- [ ] Specific time/effort data
- [ ] Has tried manual solutions
- [ ] Has budget allocated
- [ ] Timeline < 60 days
- [ ] Open to custom AI engineering (not just configured tools)
- [ ] References competitors or peers using AI

## Discovery Questions by Industry

### Manufacturing
- "How many hours/week on scheduling/inventory?"
- "What's your defect rate? Can AI help?"
- "How do you handle supplier communication?"

### Healthcare
- "What's your front desk currently doing?"
- "How much time on appointment reminders?"
- "What's your no-show rate?"

### Restaurant
- "How many calls/week for takeout orders?"
- "What's your reservation system?"
- "How do you handle follow-ups?"

### Retail
- "What % of calls are FAQs vs real issues?"
- "How do you handle returns?"
- "What's your customer follow-up process?"
