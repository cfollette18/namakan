# Logo Prompt v4 - 3 Parallel Capsules

## The N Symbol - 3 Capsules

The N is made of 3 separate capsule/stadium shapes (rounded rectangles):

**All 3 capsules are tilted at 60 degrees (slanted)**

**Arrangement from top to bottom:**
1. **Top capsule:** Short capsule at the top, tilted 60°
2. **Middle capsule:** Longest capsule, tilted 60°, positioned in the center — this forms the main diagonal
3. **Bottom capsule:** Short capsule at the bottom, tilted 60°

**There are GAPS between each capsule** — they don't touch

**When you look at the gaps between them, they create the N shape**
