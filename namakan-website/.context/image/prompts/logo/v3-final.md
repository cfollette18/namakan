# Logo Prompt v3 - The EXACT N Shape

## The N Symbol Structure
The N is made of EXACTLY 2 pieces:

**Each piece is an "L" or "hockey stick" shape:**
- One arm is vertical (goes up or down)
- One arm is diagonal (goes up-right or down-left)
- Rounded ends on the arms
- The corner is rounded on the outside, sharp on the inside

**The 2 pieces:**
1. **Piece 1 (left side):** The arm going DOWN forms the left vertical. The arm going diagonally UP-RIGHT forms the lower half of the diagonal.
2. **Piece 2 (right side):** The arm going UP forms the right vertical. The arm going diagonally DOWN-LEFT forms the upper half of the diagonal.

**Gap:** There's a DIAGONAL GAP between the two pieces running through the center.

**Result:** When you put them together, they form an N with a diagonal gap in the middle.
