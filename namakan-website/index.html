What We Build

THE FOUR THINGS WE BUILD

01

Fine-Tuned Models — AI That Knows Your Domain

We train private AI models on your proprietary data. Your industry knowledge. Your products. Your customers. Your internal processes. The result: an AI that understands YOUR domain — not just language in general.

Example: A law firm trained a model on their case history, contracts, and legal precedents. Now they have AI that drafts and reviews contracts in their specific practice areas, with their specific language and standards.

Investment $10,000–$50,000 per project
02

RAG Pipelines — AI That Knows Your Documents

We connect AI to your documents, knowledge bases, CRM, and internal systems. Your AI doesn't guess — it searches YOUR files, YOUR data, YOUR records, and gives answers grounded in what you actually know.

Example: A manufacturing company connected AI to their engineering specs, SOPs, and supplier contracts. Now their team asks AI questions about production processes and gets answers from their actual documentation — not hallucinated guesses.

Investment $5,000–$15,000 to build + $500–$2,000/month maintenance
03

Agentic Workflows — AI Workers That Do The Work

We build autonomous AI workers that complete multi-step tasks in your systems. Not chatbots that respond. AI agents that DO — processing documents, updating records, triggering actions, escalating when needed.

Example: A healthcare administrator used to spend 3 hours daily processing patient intake forms. We built an AI workflow that extracts information, validates against records, flags issues, and prepares everything for review — 10 minutes of human time per day instead of 3 hours.

Investment $5,000–$30,000 per workflow
04

Custom AI Employees — Full-Time AI Workers, Trained On Your Business

We create permanent AI workers trained exclusively on your business, integrated into your operations. Think of it as hiring an AI employee who already knows your industry, your processes, and your standards — and works 24/7.

Example: A financial advisory firm deployed a Custom AI Employee that manages client onboarding: collects documents, runs compliance checks, prepares account setup, and notifies the advisor only when human judgment is needed. The advisor focuses on relationships; the AI handles the paperwork.

Investment $2,000–$5,000/month retainer (includes initial training)
